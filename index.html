The Journey

All Scouts start out working toward their SCOUT BADGE. It doesn't take long in our Troop for them to progress through the lower ranks to FIRST CLASS. In fact, almost every new Scout in Troop 2205 earns her FIRST CLASS rank in less than a year! Once a Troop 2205 Scout attains FIRST CLASS she is guided along the path through STAR and LIFE ranks to the rank of EAGLE. This journey requires a lot of dedication and growth through the completion of merit badges, serving in leadership positions, and rendering service to the local community through projects and special activities. Earning the rank of EAGLE SCOUT is a feat recognized world-wide.
